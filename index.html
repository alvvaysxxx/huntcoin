About Community Hunt Coin (CHC)

Community Hunt Coin (CHC) is a cryptocurrency designed to support and incentivize activity within online communities. CHC allows community members to earn coins for their contributions to discussions, content, and interactions with others. This innovative solution fosters the growth and development of communities while creating an ecosystem where participants can be rewarded for their digital contributions.

Burning

On a quarterly basis, the HUNT COIN team will allocate 20% of its earnings towards repurchasing $HC tokens and permanently removing them from circulation through burning.
